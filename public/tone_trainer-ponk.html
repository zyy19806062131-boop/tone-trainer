How to read the tone cells: each Chinese character has its own cell showing the standard tone shape: Tone 1 is high and level, Tone 2 rises, Tone 3 dips, Tone 4 falls, and the neutral tone is a dot. Pinyin and tone numbers appear below each cell; brighter cells mark stressed syllables, and the current syllable lights up during playback. Listen once in Normal mode, then switch to Slow and shadow the sentence cell by cell.
